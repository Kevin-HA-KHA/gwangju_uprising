Conclusion

Worth noting

This documentary explores the crucial role of images in conveying history, focusing on the tragic events in Gwangju, South Korea. Through powerful footage and poignant testimonies, we delve into the Korean student uprising, confronted by brutal repression.

2024년 5월 광주에서
May 2024 at Gwangju

A legacy to remember

These images, true windows into the past, reveal the violence inflicted and the bravery of citizens fighting for democracy. They remind us of the importance of preserving the memory of these events to prevent such tragedies from happening again. However, this documentary also shows that truth is often complex and fragmented, shaped by a multitude of narratives and perspectives.

It is these multiple truths that, when brought together, contribute to a collective understanding of history.

In Conclusion ...

This project is not just an exploration of the past but an invitation to reflect on how we can, through images and testimonies, capture and transmit the essential lessons of our shared history.
